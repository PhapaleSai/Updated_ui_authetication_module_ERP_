 
Database Schema based on my ERD: 
1. Authentication & Academic (External Dependencies) 
These tables are typically managed by Authentication' modules but are essential for your Foreign Keys. 
 
user: Stores credentials and base roles. 
 
course: Stores college offerings (Fields: course_id, c_name, duration, level). (p. 1) 
 
2.  Brochure Phase 
The entry point for any applicant. 
 
brochure_request: Captures the initial intent and course selection. 
o 
bro_id (PK) 
o 
user_id (FK) 
o 
course_id (FK) 
o 
bro_status (e.g., 'Pending', 'Payment Done') 
o 
created_at, updated_at (p. 1) 
 
bro_payment: Specific to the ₹200 fee. 
o 
payment_id (PK) 
o 
bro_id (FK) 
o 
amount (Constant: 200) 
o 
status, paid_at (p. 1) 
 
3. Application & Details 
Once the brochure is paid, this central entity is created. 
 
application: The "Heart" of your module. 
o 
app_id (PK) 
o 
user_id (FK) 
o 
bro_id (FK) 
o 
course_id (FK) 
o 
current_status (Current state of the app) 
o 
is_active, submission_date, admission_year (p. 1) 
 
applicant_details: Extended personal information. 
o 
applicant_id (PK) 
o 
app_id (FK) 
o 
full_name, dob, gender, blood_group, marital_status 
o 
adhar_no, nationality, address, caste_category 
o 
disability_status, scholarship_required, annual_income (p. 1) 
 
parent_details: Captures multiple parents/guardians for one application. 
o 
parent_id (PK), app_id (FK) 
o 
parent_type (Mother/Father/Guardian), name, occupation, email, mob_no (p. 1) 
 
education_details: History from 10th grade onwards. 
o 
edu_id (PK), app_id (FK) 
o 
qualification_level (10th, 12th, UG), institution_name, university_board 
o 
passout_year, percentage_result, stream, attempt_count (p. 1) 
 
4.Fee payment of admission confirmation 
 
payment: For the final Admission Fee (pre-approval). 
o 
payment_id (PK), app_id (FK), amount, status, paid_at (p. 1) 
 
5. Documents & Verification 
 
documents: Physical file tracking. 
o 
doc_id (PK), app_id (FK) 
o 
document_type (Caste, Marksheet, LC), file_path, file_type, file_size (p. 1) 
 
document_verification: Admin-side status per document. 
o 
verification_id (PK), document_id (FK), admin_id (FK) 
o 
status (Verified/Rejected), remarks (p. 1) 
 
6. Status Tracking & Admin Review 
 
application_status_log: Your "State Machine" to track every change. 
o 
status_log_id (PK), app_id (FK) 
o 
changed_by (User ID), changed_role (Admin/Applicant) 
o 
status_id (FK to application_status), remark, changed_at (p. 1) 
 
admin_review: Records formal actions. 
o 
review_id (PK), app_id (FK), admin_id (FK) 
o 
action (Approve/Revision Required), remarks, timestamp (p. 1) 
 
7. Finalization 
 
enrollment: The end of the lifecycle. 
o 
en_id (PK), app_id (FK), en_status, en_date (p. 1)
